Project 2: All Time Favourite Movies

Harjit's All Time Favourite Movies

My top 5 movies of all time


The Prestige

The acting performances of Christian Bale and Hugh Jackman are wonderful, as well as the jumbled story telling, very curious.

Inglorious Basterds

Loved the style of film making, the alternate take on history, and amazing acting performances

The Dark Knight

Again, one of the greatest performances ever by Heath Ledger, and the storytelling is amazing.

Interstellar

A visually stunning movie with a thought-provoking story and an incredible score by Hans Zimmer.

Inception

A mind-bending thriller with a complex narrative and outstanding performances by the cast.
